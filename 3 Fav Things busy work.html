3 Favorite Animals

The Giraffe

Giraffes are plant eating docile animals, that become scared easily. seemingly unconventional, their long necks allow them to eat the leaves of tall trees in the savanah where trees are sparcely located.

The hedgehog

The hedgehog, while cute is a dangerous pet to the unwary holder. Their spines on their back is their main source of protections to predators. Many consider them to be a type of "pest control", because they feed on common pests and insects.

The Elephant

These gigantic grey creatures, while intimidating , are rather docile in nature. Their diet consists of mainly plants in rainforests. Similar to society, elephants create a social structure that can be compared to a family. contrasting human family structure, the mother is the leader of the herd of elephants.
